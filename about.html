About Me

My name is Samantha Sikora, and I am an artist specializing in digital illustration and mixed traditional art. I specialize in realism, and capturing moments of time of people or spaces. Based on the medium I use, I explore their stories using color, texture, and lighting.

Education: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Digital Arts and Sciences

Tools I have used: Acrylic paint, Charcoal pencils, Graphite pencils, 3D software engine like Maya and substance painter for textures.
